All Calculators

Explore budgeting and loan tools designed to help you understand your cash flow, compare options, and make more confident money decisions. Each calculator opens in a simple, mobile-friendly layout.

Smart Budget Percentages

Category: Budgeting

Enter your monthly take-home income to see how a typical household might allocate spending across major categories, then compare that to a budget-conscious target plan. Use it to spot where your budget may be out of balance.

Simple Loan Calculator

Category: Loans

Enter a loan amount, interest rate, and term in years to estimate your monthly payment, total amount paid, and total interest for a standard fixed-rate loan.

Missing Loan Term

Category: Loans

Know any three of your loan’s values—principal, interest rate, number of payments, or payment amount—and this calculator will estimate the missing one using standard amortization formulas. Helpful for understanding payoff options and “what if” scenarios.

Accelerated Debt Payoff (Debt Snowball)

Category: Loans

Enter multiple debts plus an extra monthly amount to see how the “debt snowball” method can speed up your payoff. Compare your current combined payment and payoff timeline to an accelerated plan that rolls each paid-off payment into the next debt.

Debt Payoff Goal

Category: Loans

Enter your current balance, interest rate, and a payoff goal date to see the monthly payment needed, months until payoff, and interest cost along the way.
