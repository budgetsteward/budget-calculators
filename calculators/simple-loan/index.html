Simple Loan Calculator

Enter a loan amount, annual interest rate, and term in years to see an estimated monthly payment and total interest paid over the life of the loan.

Inputs

Total amount you plan to borrow, before interest.

Yearly interest rate on the loan. Enter as a percentage, such as 7.5 for 7.5%.

Length of the loan in years. You can enter decimals (for example, 7.5 years).

Results

Enter your values and select Calculate to see your estimated payment and total interest.

The payment shown is an estimate for a fixed-rate loan with equal monthly payments. It does not include taxes, insurance, or other fees your lender may add.

Click an info icon next to an input to see tips for that field.

How this calculator works

This tool uses the standard fixed-rate loan amortization formula. The monthly interest rate is the annual rate divided by 12, and the monthly payment is computed so that the loan is paid in full after the selected number of months. Total interest equals the sum of all payments minus the original principal.
